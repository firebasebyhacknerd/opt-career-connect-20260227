# OPT Career Connect - Complete Project Plan

## ğŸ¯ Executive Summary

**OPT Career Connect** is an AI-powered job platform specifically designed for international students on OPT, CPT, and F1 visa status. Our mission is to bridge the gap between talented international students and US employers through intelligent matching, resume optimization, and comprehensive career guidance.
